The Core Idea

IDWT rests on five postulates — the first of which replaces many.

  1. Ψ∞ is a Dirac spinor field on an infinite-dimensional manifold M_∞. It is the only fundamental object.
  2. Our observable 3D universe is a fixed slice through this space: ψ_obs = Ψ∞(r, ξ⁰, t).
  3. The hidden dimensions are not compactified — they are macroscopic. Standard exclusions of macroscopic extra dimensions assume graviton propagation; IDWT has no gravitons, so those constraints do not apply.
  4. Mass is the cumulative count of hidden microstates accessible at a given excitation level.
  5. Gravity emerges from the back-reaction of |Ψ∞|² on the 4D projected geometry.

The mass formula is combinatorial, not fitted. Mode n in sector d has mass proportional to the binomial coefficient S(n,d) — the dimension of the space of degree-n polynomials in d variables:

m = m_scale(d) × S(n, d)   where   S(n,d) = C(n+d−1, d)

The sector dimensions d ∈ {2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10} are not chosen — they are the unique sequence generated by the Hopf fibration chain over the normed division algebras ℝ, ℂ, ℍ, 𝕆, terminating at d = 10 by Hurwitz's theorem. The spinor type in each sector is forced by the Clifford algebra periodicity (Bott periodicity, mod 8) and determines which quantum numbers attach to each mode.

Why It Works

  • a
    Sector dimensions are forced, not chosen. The Hopf fibration chain over ℝ, ℂ, ℍ, 𝕆 uniquely produces d = 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10. Each d corresponds to a particle family. There are no free choices in the sector assignment.
  • b
    The generation law is a combinatorial theorem. The hockey-stick identity S(n,d) = S(n,d−1) + S(n−1,d) forces n(μ) = n(charm) + n(ν₂) = 20 + 15 = 35, and every other generation relation. The lepton spectrum is not fitted — it is the unique fixed point of the combinatorial map applied to seeds {1, 4}.
  • c
    One measured constant, the full spectrum. With me as the single empirical input, the theory derives all quark and lepton masses, W, Z, Higgs, ρ meson, sin²θW, Fermi constant, and more. The W mass follows from g₂₂ = (S(n_s,3)−n_u)² × S(n_u−1,4)/2 = 722.5, derived from the seed n_s = 4 alone.
  • d
    The spinor structure of Ψ∞ derives the quantum number package. Spin-½, Fermi statistics, the chirality of the weak force (from the Kähler form on CP² and CP³), and the prediction that neutrinos are Dirac fermions (Majorana spinors forbidden in d=5 by Clifford periodicity) all follow from the spinor structure. Mass values are determined by m = m_scale_d × S(n,d); quantum numbers are determined by the spinor bundle on each sector manifold.
