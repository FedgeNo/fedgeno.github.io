The Core Idea

IDWT is built from five postulates.

  1. Reality is a single complex wave Ψ∞ on an infinite-dimensional manifold.
  2. Our observable 3D universe is a brane-like slice through this space.
  3. The hidden dimensions are not compactified Planck-scale curls, they are macroscopic.
  4. What we call mass is a scaled count of hidden microstates.
  5. Gravity emerges as the curvature of Ψ∞.

This gives a combinatorial mass rule: mass scales with cumulative states on d-dimensional hidden sectors.

m = m_scale,d × S(n,d)   where   S(n,d) = C(n+d-1, d)
